infant birth weight.â€ This finding was based on 9 machine learning
models.

predictors. There is no specific variable in the dataset for preterm
birth. In the dataset, relevant predictors for the outcome `bwt` would

- `babysex`: babyâ€™s sex (male = 1, female = 2)
- `mheight`: motherâ€™s height (inches)
- `ppbmi`: motherâ€™s pre-pregnancy BMI
